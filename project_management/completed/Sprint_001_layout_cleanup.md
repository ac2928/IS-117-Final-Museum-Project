# SPRINT_001 — Layout Cleanup & Design Foundation
**Status:** Completed
**Date:** March–April 2026

## Goals

Establish the visual and structural foundation of the museum site coming out of the midterm review:
- Choose and implement a clear design style
- Define the exhibit structure and narrative flow
- Build the homepage and main sections
- Make the site feel like a museum, not just a webpage
